# A tour of the interface

Once you're signed in, here's what you're looking at.

![The EchoMode interface after login](/images/manual/getting-started/interface-overview.png)

<p class="manual-caption">The EchoMode workspace: chat list on the left, the conversation in the center, and drift signals in the header.</p>

## Where things live

| Destination | What you'll find |
|---|---|
| **Dataroom** | Your folders and documents. See [Datarooms & Documents](/manual/datarooms/create-a-dataroom). |
| **Agent library** | Built-in agents, plus any you build. See [Agents](/manual/agents/agent-library). |
| **Logs** | A per-chat history of drift scores and states. See [Reading the signals](/manual/understanding-drift/reading-the-signals). |
| **Settings** | Profile, Security, Usage, and Workspace management. See [Account](/manual/account/profile-and-avatar). |
