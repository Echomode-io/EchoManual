# Create your account

You can join EchoMode with a **Google account** or with an **email and password**. Both take
less than a minute.

## Sign up with Google

On the sign-up screen, choose **Sign up with Google** and approve the Google prompt. You'll
land in EchoMode with your name and photo already filled in.

## Sign up with an email and password

1. **Open the sign-up screen.** Find the email form below the **Sign up with Google** button.
2. **Enter your work email** in the **Work email** field (for example, `you@company.com`).
3. **Create a password.** It must be **10–128 characters** and include an **uppercase letter, a
   lowercase letter, a number, and a special character**. A strength meter shows your progress
   as you type. Retype it in the confirm field.
4. **Agree to the terms.** Tick **"I agree to Echomode's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy."**
   You can leave the product-updates checkbox on or turn it off.
5. **Create your account.** Select **Create account**. The button changes to **Creating
   account…**, then you're taken into the app.

## Log in

To return later, open the login screen and either choose **Sign in with Google** or enter your
**email and password** and select **Log in**.

::: warning
EchoMode has no self-serve "forgot password" link. If you signed up with Google, just sign in
with Google. If you use an email and password and get locked out, ask a workspace admin to
reset it for you (see [Roles & permissions](/manual/teams/roles-and-permissions)).
:::

## First-run personalization

Some workspaces show a short setup right after sign-up that tailors EchoMode to you — your
industry, your role, the tone you prefer, and how strictly the assistant should stay on-topic.
If you see it, pick the options that fit; you can change agent behavior later from the
[Agent library](/manual/agents/agent-library).

::: info
This personalization step depends on your workspace's setup, so it may not appear in every
account.
:::
