# Viewing a document

EchoMode gives you two ways to look at a document, plus a download.

## Quick view

**Single-click** a document to open the **quick-view** panel on the side. It shows the
document's name, a preview, and its details — handy for a fast look without leaving the folder.

## Full view

**Double-click** the document, or choose **Open full view** from the quick-view panel, to open
it full screen. EchoMode renders the document's text, a PDF preview, or the image, and gives you
a **Download** button for the original file.

## From a chat citation

When the assistant answers from your documents, it adds numbered citations like `[1]` and `[2]`.
Selecting a citation brings you here, to the cited document, with the relevant passage
highlighted — so you can always check the source. See
[How search works](/manual/knowledge-base/how-search-works).
