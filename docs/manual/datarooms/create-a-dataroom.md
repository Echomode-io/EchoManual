# Create a dataroom

The **Dataroom** is where your documents live. It's a workspace of **folders** and the documents
inside them — the same documents you can later bring into a chat as a
[knowledge base](/manual/knowledge-base/how-search-works).

Open it from the **Dataroom** destination in the app. You don't create a "dataroom" as a
separate object — you organize your space by creating folders and uploading documents into them.

![The Dataroom page with its folder tree](/images/manual/datarooms/dataroom-overview.png)

<p class="manual-caption">The Dataroom: your folder tree and the documents inside.</p>

## Create a folder

1. **Select Create.** In the Dataroom, choose **Create** to make a new folder.
2. **Name it.** Enter a **name** for the folder.
3. **Choose where it goes.** Pick the parent folder it should sit inside, then confirm. EchoMode
   confirms with *"Created &lt;name&gt;."*

::: info
Your workspace starts with folders at the **organization**, **team**, and **personal** levels,
created for you automatically. Personal folders are yours; team and organization folders are
shared according to your [role](/manual/teams/roles-and-permissions).
:::
