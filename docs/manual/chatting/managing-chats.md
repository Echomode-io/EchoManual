# Managing chats

All your conversations live in the **sidebar** on the left.

## Your chat list

The sidebar lists your recent chats, **newest first**. A chat's title comes from your first
message, so conversations are easy to recognize at a glance. Select any chat to reopen it and
keep going.

![The chat sidebar](/images/manual/chatting/chat-sidebar.png)

<p class="manual-caption">The sidebar keeps your recent chats, newest first.</p>

## Rename and delete

EchoMode can rename and remove chats. Where these controls appear can vary by build — look for
them on a chat row (often on hover, or via a small menu).

::: info
If you don't see rename or delete controls in your sidebar yet, they may not be surfaced in
your version.
:::

## Revisit a chat's drift history

Every conversation keeps a detailed record of how on-topic it stayed. Open the **Logs** page to
see a chat's alignment scores over time, round by round. See
[Reading the signals](/manual/understanding-drift/reading-the-signals) for how to read it.
