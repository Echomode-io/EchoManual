# Org settings

Your workspace is organized in three levels:

**Organization → Teams → Members.** Everyone belongs to the organization; teams group people
within it; and a person can be on more than one team.

You manage all of this under **Settings → Workspace**.

![Organization and workspace settings](/images/manual/teams/org-settings.png)

<p class="manual-caption">Workspace settings: your teams and the people on them. The organization name is set under Settings › General.</p>

## What you can manage

- **Rename the organization** — update your organization's name under **Settings → General**.
- **Create and remove teams** — organize people into teams.
- **Manage team membership** — add or remove people from a team and set their team role.
- **Manage members** — invite, change roles, deactivate, reactivate, or remove people (see
  [Roles & permissions](/manual/teams/roles-and-permissions)).

::: info
The full members and teams management screens live in workspace settings. If a control isn't
visible in your build yet, it may not be surfaced in your version.
:::

::: tip
Want to see your own role and the teams you're on? Open **Settings → Profile**, where your
workspace membership is listed. See [Profile & avatar](/manual/account/profile-and-avatar).
:::
