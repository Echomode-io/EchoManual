# What is EchoMode

EchoMode is an AI chat app where you talk to domain **agents** — an Engineer, a Legal
expert, a Marketing specialist, and more. Each agent answers in its own voice and stays
focused on its subject.

What makes EchoMode different is the **Echo engine**. After every reply, EchoMode quietly
scores how on-topic the conversation is and nudges the assistant back toward the agent's
subject when the discussion starts to wander. This is called **drift detection and
correction**, and it runs in the background the whole time you chat.
