# Usage limits

Your workspace runs on a plan with set limits. Track where you stand in **Settings → Usage**.

## Your plan

EchoMode workspaces are on the **Free**, **Pro**, or **Max** plan. The Usage panel tells you
which one you're on. On the **Pro** and **Max** plans, **inference usage resets each billing cycle**. The **Free** plan has a fixed inference allowance with no billing cycle.

![The Usage settings panel](/images/manual/account/usage-panel.png)

<p class="manual-caption">Settings › Usage: your plan and the three usage meters.</p>

## What's tracked

| Meter | What it measures |
|---|---|
| **Members** | How many people are in your organization, against your plan's limit |
| **Data storage** | How many pages of documents you've stored, against your limit |
| **Inference** | How much of each person's inference credit (in dollars) has been used this cycle |

Each meter fills up as you go. It shows **orange** while you have headroom, turns **yellow** as
you near the limit (around 80%), and turns **red** when you reach it.

::: info
Approaching a limit? An Org Admin can review the plan, and you can free up document storage
by [deleting documents](/manual/datarooms/upload-manage-documents) you no longer need.
:::
