# Profile & avatar

Your profile is in **Settings → Profile**. It's where you set how your name and photo appear
across the workspace.

## Edit your name

Update your **first name** and **last name**, then choose **Save profile**. Your **display name**
(shown in the sidebar and across the workspace) and your **email** are built from your account
and shown read-only here.

![The Profile settings panel](/images/manual/account/profile-panel.png)

<p class="manual-caption">Settings › Profile: your photo, name, and account details.</p>

## Change your photo

Select **Change photo** and pick an image. EchoMode accepts **JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF, under
5 MB**. You can remove your photo to go back to your initials.

## See your workspace membership

Your Profile panel also shows the workspace you belong to, your role in it, and the teams you're
on. To manage the organization itself, see [Org settings](/manual/teams/org-settings).

::: info
Need to close your account? Account deletion is handled by support — contact
[support@echomode.io](mailto:support@echomode.io) and they'll remove your account and data.
:::
